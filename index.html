X-Ray Classification

The NIH X-Ray dataset is one of the largest public datasets for machine learning on medical data. For this problem, I trained a convolutional neural network to diagnose only based on the X-ray image.

The main issues associated with this dataset is the sparsity of the labels. In most cases, a person does not have a disease, and some diseases are very rare in the dataset. This means that the dataset suffers from a sparse signal and high levels of data imbalance. To solve this issue, I attempted to use a weighted loss function that gives more weight to the diseases that are less common in the dataset.
